How it works

From pantry to plate in three exchanges

No forms, no filters, no scrolling through ads. Just tell SmartChef what you want to cook.

01 / ASK

Describe your craving

Name a dish, an ingredient, or a mood — "something light with lentils" works as well as "chana masala."

02 / REFINE

Adjust on the fly

Swap ingredients, change servings, or ask for a quicker version. The agent rewrites the recipe in place.

03 / COOK

Follow along, step by step

Get numbered steps with timing built in, so you can ask "what's next" without losing your place.
